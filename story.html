In a few minutes, I will show you my story that I wrote for you.

But first, I of course have to tell you about it.

Jess was just 13 when her family left her, abandoned in the forest. A talking wolf soon adopts her and Jess grows into her habitat in the forest.

But then, humans start hunting for the animals in the forest and the wolf starts beginning to distrust Jess...

The only choice she has is to protect her animal friends and her habitat.

The story is not finished yet, but I hope you like it so far.
